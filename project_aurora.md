## Success Criteria

*What does done look like? This is the unambiguous finish line.*

By December 31, 2026, Ember Horizon Studios is:

- A legally registered Missouri LLC with Articles of Organization, EIN, and signed Operating Agreement on file
- Holding a dedicated business bank account (BOI filing exempt per FinCEN 2026 rule change)
- Registered as a developer on Steam and itch.io with tax and payout configuration complete
- Operating under a finalized studio name and baseline brand identity
- Running at least one game concept in active development (prototype or pre-prototype phase)
- Publicly present via at least one active channel (devlog, newsletter, or social media account)
