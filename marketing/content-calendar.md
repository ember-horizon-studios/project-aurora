## Solo Dev Posting Rhythm

**Recommended cadence for a solo founder with no game in active production yet:**

- **X:** 3 posts/week (Mon / Wed / Fri or Tue / Thu / Sat — pick one and stick to it)
- **YouTube Shorts / TikTok:** Hold until you have something to show on screen. Don't manufacture video content at this stage.
- **Batch drafting:** Write the week's posts in one session (Sunday evening or Monday morning). ~30 min/week.
- **Rule:** Never go dark more than 7 days. One honest post beats silence.

The calendar below is a starting scaffold. Adjust dates and swap topics as reality dictates — the cadence matters more than the specific subjects.

## Notes

- **Hashtags:** #indiedev and #gamedev on threads and milestone posts. Don't over-tag short posts — it reads as noise.
- **Replies:** Spend 10–15 min after each post engaging with replies. Early community is built in the comments, not just the posts.
- **Devlog cadence:** Aim for a devlog thread (like 001) every 3–4 weeks. They're the heaviest posts but do the most work.
- **Video unlock trigger:** When the first game concept is locked and you have something on screen — even a rough prototype — activate YouTube Shorts / TikTok. Until then, hold.
- **Adjust freely:** This is a scaffold, not a contract. A post that feels false because the moment isn't right does more damage than silence for a day.
