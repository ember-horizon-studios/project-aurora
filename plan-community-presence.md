## Decision Rules

- **Primary channel choice (decided 2026-05-06):** X (Twitter) as primary — largest overlap of players, press, and industry; low production overhead for daily devlog posts. YouTube Shorts and TikTok as secondary video channels — same raw video uploaded natively to both (no re-posts of watermarked clips). Bluesky deprioritized — strong dev community but wrong audience for wishlist goals. This three-platform stack balances reach, wishlist pipeline, and solo dev overhead.

- **Domain overlap with P2-M3:** If P2-M3 closes domain registration first, mark the domain and email tasks in P3-M2 as `resolved via P2-M3` and close them without duplication. Log in both plans' Review Notes.

- **Profile image:** A placeholder (studio initials or simple logo sketch) is acceptable to close M1. Final identity asset is required to close M2. Do not block M1 on polished artwork.

- **Devlog platform:** Primary channel devlog is required for M3. A secondary cross-post (e.g., itch.io devlog, Steam blog) is optional and does not block M3 closure.

- **Content calendar scope:** M3 content plan covers 4 weeks only — enough runway without over-planning. Revisit cadence in the Weekly Review after first post ships.
